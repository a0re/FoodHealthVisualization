The chart below illustrates the trend in life expectancy among OECD nations over time. Overall, life expectancy has increased consistently. In recent years, most OECD countries boast life expectancies exceeding 80 years, with only a few exceptions. This indicates a general improvement in health and longevity. Our subsequent visualizations will explore whether factors such as alcohol consumption and various types of food consumption influence the changing life expectancy values in OECD countries.
