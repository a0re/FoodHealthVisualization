Let's shift our focus to the food consumption trends of individual OECD countries from 1995 to 2020. By selecting a country from the "Choose country" dropdown, you can observe specific trends. Overall, the supply of fat and protein has gradually increased over this period, while sugar supply has experienced slight fluctuations. However, in recent years, particularly in 2019 and 2020, the supply of protein has been significantly lower compared to fat and sugar. This imbalance is concerning, as it can contribute to various health issues and potentially lead to a lower life expectancy in OECD countries.
