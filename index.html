Welcome! We are dedicated to analysing non-medical determinants of health in OECD countries from 1995 to 2020. Our goal is to explore how lifestyle choices like alcohol consumption and dietary habits impact life expectancy, uncover relationships between these choices and national health outcomes, and examine their influence on longevity in OECD populations.
By providing insights into the effects of lifestyle choices on health and longevity, we aim to raise awareness about the importance of healthy behaviours for overall well-being. Join us in exploring these significant correlations and patterns through our visualisations!
