The previous analysis provided insights into how life expectancy in OECD countries has changed over time. The current visualization highlights the top ten OECD nations with the highest average alcohol consumption from 1995 to 2020. This comparison helps to assess the potential impact of high alcohol consumption on life expectancy. France stands out as the highest consumer of alcohol, with a consumption rate significantly above the OECD average. Recent life expectancy data indicates that this high level of alcohol consumption may play a crucial role in influencing life expectancy in these countries.
